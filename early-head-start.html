Early Head Start

Give your child a strong start in life.

Early Head Start is a no-cost, federally funded program that helps infants, toddlers, and expectant families build the foundation for lifelong learning and success.

What is Early Head Start?

Early Head Start is designed to nurture children during their most important years. It is the only federal program specifically focused on children from birth to age 3 and expectant families.

Research shows that 90% of a child's brain develops before age 5, and the most critical period is from birth to age 3. EHS provides comprehensive support to help your child reach important developmental milestones while strengthening your entire family.

Services are delivered through quality childcare at centers like Nana's Cozy Corner, where your child receives nurturing care in a safe, home-like environment.

Who qualifies?

  • Families with children from birth to age 3, up to age 2.5 at enrollment, who meet income guidelines
  • Pregnant women who want support during pregnancy and after birth
  • Families receiving SSI, TANF, or SNAP, who may automatically qualify regardless of income
  • Children in foster care or families experiencing homelessness
  • Families earning at or below Federal Poverty Level guidelines
  • Children with disabilities or developmental delays
