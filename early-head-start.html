How EHS Helps

What is Early Head Start?

Early Head Start is a federally funded program for eligible families during pregnancy and the first three years of a child's life. At Nana's Cozy Corner, EHS support pairs high-quality childcare with family partnership and developmental care.

1

Early learning and care

Children receive nurturing daily routines, play-based learning, healthy meals, and a safe classroom environment.

2

Health and development

Families may receive support with screenings, milestones, referrals, and services that help children grow with confidence.

3

Family partnership

EHS works with parents and caregivers on goals, communication, family stability, and next steps for school readiness.

Who May Qualify

EHS is designed for families who could use extra support early.

Eligibility is reviewed through program guidelines, family circumstances, and available openings. A pre-screen helps Nana's team understand your situation before next steps are confirmed.

We review each family's situation with care.
Age or pregnancy

Expectant families and children from birth to age 3 may be eligible.

Income or benefits

Families receiving SNAP, TANF, or SSI may qualify, and income guidelines may apply.

Housing or foster care

Families experiencing housing instability, homelessness, foster care, or kinship care may receive priority.

Developmental needs

Children with disabilities, delays, or additional support needs may be eligible for services.

How It Works
