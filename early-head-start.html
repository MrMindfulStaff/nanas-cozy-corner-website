Early Head Start

No-cost support may be available for eligible families.

Early Head Start supports eligible families with children ages 0-3 and expectant families. This page helps you understand common indicators before submitting a pre-screen.

Who can apply?

Families with infants, toddlers, children ages 0-3, and expectant families may qualify based on income or other priority indicators. The pre-screen does not guarantee eligibility, but it helps Nana's Cozy Corner understand your situation and guide your next step.

  • Income eligibility interest or uncertainty
  • SNAP, TANF, SSI, foster, or kinship status
  • Housing instability or temporary housing
  • Expectant family support needs
  • Health, dental, vision, hearing, developmental, and family engagement support needs

Supportive, not bureaucratic.

The goal is to make the process feel clear and human. Share what you know, choose “unsure” when needed, and Nana's team can follow up.
