See the center and ask your questions in person.

A tour helps families understand the space, routines, program fit, and enrollment next steps before making a decision.

What the tour covers

Tour requests help Nana's team prepare for your visit. We will review your child's age, your care needs, possible start timing, and any Early Head Start questions.

  • Classroom environment and daily routines
  • Age group fit and program availability
  • Parent communication expectations
  • Enrollment documents and next steps
  • Early Head Start questions if applicable

Before you arrive

Bring any questions about schedule, care needs, eligibility, and start dates. The goal is to make the next step feel clear.
