About Nana

Meet the owner behind Nana's Cozy Corner.

At Nana's Cozy Corner, early childhood education isn't just our business. It is our legacy.

Ms. Kelly leads with education, family legacy, and hands-on experience.

Our center is led by an owner with a Master's degree in education and more than 25 years of hands-on experience, representing a significant part of our team's 70+ combined years in early childhood care.

She comes from a family of child educators, where nurturing young minds has always been more than a profession. It is a calling.

We believe the earliest years of a child's life are the most influential. When children are supported, encouraged, and positively guided early on, they are far more likely to grow into confident, curious, and capable individuals. That belief shapes everything we do, from how our classrooms are structured, to how we interact with each child, to how we partner with families.

At the heart of our center is a simple promise: to treat every child as if they were our own, and to give them the strongest possible foundation for a bright future.
