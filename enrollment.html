Enrollment path

What happens after you submit interest?

Enrollment starts with a simple request. From there, Nana's team helps your family understand availability, classroom fit, documents, and support options.

1

Submit interest

Share parent contact details, child information, program age group, timing, and schedule needs.

2

We contact you

Nana's team confirms care needs, preferred contact method, and any questions you already have.

3

Tour + review

Families can visit the center and discuss Early Head Start, transportation, and classroom fit if applicable.

4

Placement next steps

Complete documents, review start date, and prepare your child for a warm first day.

What We Review

We use your request to understand the right care path.

The enrollment request is not a final application. It gives our team the details needed to contact you with helpful next steps.

Tours help families feel confident before care begins.
Child and program fit

We review child ages, classroom group, care type, and the number of children needing care.

Schedule and start date

Desired start date, full-time or part-time care, and drop-off and pick-up timing help us plan availability.

Support needs

Transportation, Early Head Start interest, payment type, and family notes help us guide you to the right next step.

Parent communication

Phone, email, and preferred contact details help our team follow up quickly and clearly.
