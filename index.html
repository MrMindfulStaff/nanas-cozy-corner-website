Early Head Start

No-cost support may be available for eligible families.

Early Head Start supports eligible families with children ages 0-3 and expectant families. Nana's Cozy Corner can help you start a simple pre-screening so the right team can review fit, eligibility, and next steps.

  • Income, SNAP, TANF, SSI, foster, kinship, and housing indicators can help determine eligibility.
  • Eligible families may receive health, dental, vision, hearing, developmental, and family engagement support.
  • The process is designed to feel supportive, clear, and parent-friendly.

Cozy Circle Referral Program

You earn when they stay.

Current parents, staff members, and approved community partners can refer families to Nana's Cozy Corner. Qualified referrals may earn a $750 bonus after 60 consecutive days of enrollment.

$750 referral bonus

Early Head Start eligible referrals may qualify for a bonus boost after eligibility confirmation.

How it works

  1. Refer a family using the referral form.
  2. We contact the family and review care needs.
  3. If they enroll and stay 60 consecutive days, the bonus is reviewed.
