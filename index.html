Proud EHS Partnership

Extra support for eligible young families.

Through our Early Head Start partnership, eligible families with children ages 0-3 may receive additional support focused on healthy development, family engagement, screenings, meals, and early learning.

Check Eligibility
  • High-quality early learning
  • Health & development screenings
  • Nutritious meals and snacks
  • Support for children with disabilities
  • Parenting support and education
  • Strong family engagement
