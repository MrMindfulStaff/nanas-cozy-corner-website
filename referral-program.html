Eligibility

  • Child must be enrolled full-time
  • Child must remain active for 60 consecutive calendar days
  • No pending withdrawal or termination notice
  • Referrer must be a current parent, staff member, or approved community partner

Program safeguards

  • Bonuses paid only after the retention milestone
  • EHS bonus paid only after eligibility confirmation
  • Maximum of 2 referral payouts per person per quarter
