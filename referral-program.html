Cozy Circle Referral Program

You earn when they stay.

Current parents, staff members, and approved community partners can refer families to Nana's Cozy Corner.

$750 referral bonus

Qualified referrals may earn a $750 bonus after 60 consecutive days of enrollment. Early Head Start eligible referrals may qualify for a bonus boost after eligibility confirmation.

Who can refer?

Current parents, staff members, and approved community partners.

How it works

  1. Refer a family using the form below.
  2. We contact the referred family and review care needs.
  3. If they enroll and stay 60 consecutive days, the bonus is reviewed.
